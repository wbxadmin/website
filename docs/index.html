How it works

Three steps to verifiable trust without exposing private data.

1) Human verification

A licensed professional (notary/CPA/attorney) verifies the subject and signs an attestation.

2) PQ certificate issuance

Vericustos validates the attestation and issues a PQ-native certificate with transparency anchoring.

3) Live verification via Orb

Users prove possession of their private key on demand; the Orb shows real-time trust status (Trusted, Paused, or Revoked).
